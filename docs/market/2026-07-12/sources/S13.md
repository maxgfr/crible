What teams build on OpenBB
Asset managers and hedge funds use OpenBB to build proprietary workflows on shared infrastructure.
Get in touch
### Portfolio analytics
Consolidate performance data, sector exposures, and risk metrics across asset classes into a single dashboard. Pull from your broker and internal systems to generate attribution analysis and volatility measures for internal reviews and stakeholder reporting.
### Crypto and digital assets
Aggregate tokenomics, on-chain metrics, and prediction market data from CoinGecko, DeFiLlama, Polymarket, Pyth, or any other. Monitor protocol health and cross-reference decentralized sentiment with equity and macro signals in real time.
### Macro research and scenario planning
Run CPI scenarios, track GDP forecasts, and monitor legislative bills that move markets. Layer in Congress tracking and FOMC data to stress-test assumptions and communicate trends without waiting on data teams. Build scenario models in minutes, not days.
### Compliance and operations intelligence
Query policy documents, audit reports, and vendor packets instantly. Ask Copilot for MFA policies or contract clauses and get the exact page and context. Turn multi-day reviews into focused sprints, with full audit trails.
### Alternative data integration
Bring shipping fuel prices, freight indices, real estate signals, or consumer spend data into your workflow. Connect alternative feeds like ICE bunker pricing directly to your research to model transport costs and inflation proxies without switching platforms.

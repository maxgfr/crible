## Frequently asked questions
### Do I need to enter credit card details to try Stockopedia?
Yes. But don’t worry, your card won’t be charged until the end of your two week free trial, which you can easily cancel without needing to contact with us.
### What do I get in my free trial?
Full access to all of Stockopedia’s tools and features, plus access to data on all of the listed companies in the regions you have selected.
### What if I change my mind?
You can cancel at any point within your trial and your card won’t be charged. If you forget to cancel and are charged, you’re protected by our 30-day money-back guarantee. Simply get in touch with our online customer support within 30 days of your first payment and we’ll issue a full refund, no questions asked.
### How do I get started?
That all depends on your investment priorities. Stockopedia's award-winning service helps with a wide variety of investment needs.
If you’re here to check the data on a specific stock, our StockReports are a good starting point, making it easy to understand the key ratios and numbers.
If you’re more interested in the general health of your portfolio, we recommend uploading your holdings to our Folio tool.
And if you’re looking for new investment ideas, our stock screening tools can help you identify some promising candidates.
Regardless of your objectives, we recommend you check out our educational environment to help you make the most of your trial.
### Will my subscription automatically renew?
Yes, your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing period (either monthly or annual, depending on what you choose). When you start your trial, you’ll be able to see your billing date and recurring billing amount.
### Can I change or cancel my subscription easily?
You can change or cancel your subscription at any time from within the Stockopedia platform. Alternatively, contact our customer support and we can do this.
### What payment methods do you support?
We currently support payment via all major credit and debit cards. All payments are processed via SSL with enterprise class security -  learn more here .
### Can I pay monthly?
You can choose to pay monthly or annually. Annual billing offers better value, whereas monthly offers more flexibility.
### How do I access Stockopedia? Do I need to install or download anything?
Stockopedia can be accessed via web browser on any device, there is no need to download or install anything. We recommend having the latest version of your browser installed for the best experience.
### What customer support do you offer?
We're dedicated to helping you succeed with our platform. While we can’t offer personalised financial advice, we have the expertise to resolve everything from simple account problems to tough financial accounting queries. Queries are handled by a range of staff from across the business, a number of which hold the Chartered Financial Analyst® designation.
Customer support is available throughout the U.K. trading day (8.30 a.m. - 4.30 p.m. Monday to Friday, excluding Bank Holidays).

# Stooq (website)
Ara Eve Leo Sal
1x
Stooq is a Polish financial website that provides free access to stock quotes , historical market data , and tools for investors , with partial translation into English for global users. [1] [2]
Established as a financial portal based in Poland , Stooq specializes in offering open, high, low, close, and volume ( OHLCV ) data across a wide range of securities , including over 21,000 (as of 2018) global stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs) from markets such as Poland, the United States , Japan , Hungary , and Germany . [1] [3] [4]
The platform is particularly noted for its comprehensive historical data archives, enabling users to download and analyze pricing information for various asset classes , and it supports multiple languages to cater to an international audience while maintaining a focus on the Warsaw Stock Exchange . [1] [5]
In addition to data services , Stooq features a mobile application that delivers real-time quotes from numerous markets , making it a valuable resource for traders and analysts seeking reliable, no-cost financial information. [2]
## History
### Founding
Stooq was launched in 2000 as a free online portal providing financial market data primarily for Polish investors. [6]
The platform originated in Poland , initially focusing on quotes and data from the Warsaw Stock Exchange (WSE) , amid challenges such as limited internet penetration in the country at the turn of the millennium. [1]
### Development and Expansion
Stooq, originally focused on the Warsaw Stock Exchange , expanded its services in the early 2000s to include coverage of international markets such as Nasdaq , NYSE , and LSE , broadening its appeal to global investors. [1] This growth aligned with the platform's commitment to providing free access to diverse financial data, supported by advertising revenue. [1]
By the 2010s , Stooq had further developed its offerings to encompass non-stock assets , including bonds , currencies , commodities , and cryptocurrencies, alongside extensive historical data archives that extend over 20 years for many assets. [1] [7] The platform also introduced multilingual support , with partial translation into English to serve a wider international audience beyond its Polish origins. [1]
Key milestones in Stooq's expansion include partnerships with data providers to ensure accuracy and real-time updates , enabling it to maintain its non-commercial model while growing its user base. [1] These developments have positioned Stooq as a comprehensive resource for investors tracking both domestic and global markets .
## Content and Services
### Market Data Coverage
Stooq provides comprehensive coverage of major stock exchanges, including the Warsaw Stock Exchange (WSE) , London Stock Exchange (LSE) , Nasdaq , and New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) , offering both real-time and historical quotes for securities traded on these platforms. [8] This includes data from additional exchanges such as NYSE MKT and LSE International, ensuring users can access quotes across a wide range of global markets. [8]
The platform offers data on diverse asset classes , encompassing stocks , bonds , currencies ( forex ), commodities , and cryptocurrencies. [1] Specifically, it includes over 285 bonds, 220 cryptocurrencies, 65 indices, 68 major currencies, 1,840 other currencies, and 62 commodity funds, among other categories. [9] Commodities data is sourced from Barchart, while cryptocurrency information is provided via CoinAPI, enhancing the breadth of available assets. [8]
Stooq delivers historical data with varying granularity levels , such as daily, hourly, and minutely intervals, covering over 21,000 global securities and ETFs , as well as nearly 2,000 currency pairs and over 220 cryptocurrencies in OHLCV format . [1] [9] This extensive archive supports in-depth analysis without requiring paid subscriptions, distinguishing Stooq as a free resource for global indices and ETFs. [1]
### Investor Tools and Features
Stooq offers a range of investor tools designed to support customized analysis and monitoring of financial markets , drawing on its extensive data resources . Users can access advanced charting tools that include technical analysis indicators and volume data to analyze price movements across various assets . [2]
The platform enables investors to create and manage watchlists and portfolio trackers, allowing for personalized tracking of selected stocks , indices, and other instruments with automatic updates for real-time quotations . [2] These features are available through both the website and its mobile application , catering to users interested in Polish and international markets such as the Warsaw Stock Exchange , Nasdaq , and NYSE . [1]
Stooq provides databases for historical price data , which investors can download in formats suitable for further analysis. [9] Additionally, news feeds and alert systems notify users of market developments and price changes, enhancing decision-making for both novice and experienced investors. [2]
While primarily focused on data-driven tools , Stooq offers multilingual support ensuring accessibility for a global audience. [2]
## Technical Aspects
### Website Design and Functionality
Stooq's website provides access to financial market information through its web interface. The platform delivers real-time stock quotes and charts, allowing users to view up-to-date data without the need for downloads or additional software in basic usage scenarios. [1]
The site features navigation for various asset types such as stocks , indices, currencies, and commodities , along with sections for forums and news. This layout supports browsing for investors . [4]
Accessibility is enhanced through multilingual support, with the primary interface in Polish and a dedicated English version available at en.stooq.com, catering to international users interested in global markets. The site also offers a companion mobile application, providing on-the-go access to quotes and data, which complements the desktop experience by ensuring usability across devices. [10] [2] [1]
### Data Access and API
Stooq provides free access to historical market data through direct CSV file downloads, available for daily, hourly, and 5-minute intervals across various asset classes including stocks , indices, ETFs , bonds , forex , and cryptocurrencies. [9] [1] [5]
These downloads can be obtained via the platform's database section, where users select specific symbols and timeframes to generate CSV files containing essential data fields such as date, open, high, low, close, and volume ( OHLCV format ). [9] [11] [5]
For programmatic access, Stooq offers undocumented API endpoints that allow integration of historical data into third-party applications , primarily through HTTP requests mimicking the CSV download functionality, such as querying by symbol and timeframe. [12] [11]
These API calls support the same OHLCV data formats as the manual downloads and are commonly used for non-US markets , though they are subject to usage limits including a low daily quota to prevent abuse, after which requests may return an "Exceeded the daily hits limit" error. [13] [12]
While explicit licensing terms are not prominently detailed, the data is provided freely for personal and research purposes without requiring formal agreements, aligning with Stooq's non-commercial model . [14]
Examples of integration include using the API via libraries like pandas-datareader in Python for retrieving historical stock prices in algorithmic trading strategies , or importing CSV data into SQL Server for quantitative analysis and backtesting . [11] [5] [1]

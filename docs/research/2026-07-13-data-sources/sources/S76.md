## Getbhavcopy
Getbhavcopy is a FREE data downloader for Indian Stock Exchanges, NSE and BSE . Getbhavcopy connects to NSE and BSE servers to download this data, so it can be considered 100% authentic. Getbhavcopy can download daily as well as historical EOD data for Equities, Indices and Futures.
Getbhavcopy exports the downloaded data in a format that is easily imported in leading technical analysis softwares like Metastock , Amibroker and Fcharts .

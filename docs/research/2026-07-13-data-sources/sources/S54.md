# SEDAR+
## About SEDAR+
SEDAR+ will be the Canadian Securities Administrators’ national system that all market participants will use for filings, disclosure, payments and information searching in Canada’s capital markets.
Once fully launched, SEDAR+ will be a simplified point of access for market participants including issuers, insiders, registrants, and investors. It will improve industry participants’ filing experience and investors’ access to disclosure information.
The CSA is building on a name that has a long legacy in Canada. SEDAR is Canada’s longest running and most well-known filing and disclosure national system for market participants. The plus (+) symbol, signifies the integration of all the CSA’s national systems, and reflects the fact that it will be a more modern, up-to-date system to better serve Canada’s market participants.
SEDAR+ portal offers:
Single portal access to all filings
Effective cyber security and privacy management
Larger scope of filings and system users
Better usability via a modern user interface, search function improvements, and harmonized processes for all filings
Better data quality through database consolidation and input standardization
Easier filing and compliance
When fully implemented, SEDAR+ will replace the National Cease Trade Order Database (CTO), the Disciplined List (DL), the System for Electronic Document Analysis and Retrieval (SEDAR), the System for Electronic Disclosure by Insiders (SEDI), the National Registration Database (NRD) and the National Registration System. It will also incorporate certain filings made in paper format or in local electronic filing systems.
For more information, refer to About SEDAR+ on the CSA website.
## Information for filers
The CSA Service Desk is currently experiencing high call volumes in respect of SEDAR+. We appreciate your patience as they work to resolve your inquiries as quickly as possible and ask that you continue to use SEDAR+ as needed.
Answers to the most common support requests can be found on the CSA website: https://www.securities-administrators.ca/about-sedar/sedarplus-support/
SEDAR+ is available 24/7 on weekdays. Occasionally, between 11:00 p.m. E.T. and 7:00 a.m. ET. system maintenance may be conducted. System maintenance is routinely conducted at various periods on weekends.
If you have a time-sensitive filing and cannot file through SEDAR+ (in Ontario) please email documents to: [email protected] (for investment funds) or [email protected] (for all other cases). For reports of exempt distribution, please continue to attempt to use SEDAR+ and do not email your documents.
Please note that documents submitted by email due to temporary hardship must still be filed through SEDAR+ as soon as possible.
For SEDAR+ system status updates, please refer to the CSA website: https://www.securities-administrators.ca/about-sedar/sedar-system-status/
